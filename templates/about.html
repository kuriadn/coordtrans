What this is

{{ SITE_NAME }} is the online survey toolkit from Fayvad Geosolutions, hosted at {{ SITE_DOMAIN }}. It brings together practical calculators and converters that survey teams use in the field and office.

Available now

Coordinate Transform (sheet) converts points between Cassini-Soldner and UTM using sheet-based affine transformation from four control points per cadastral sheet.

CRS Transform (PROJ) reprojects coordinates using default PROJ4 / EPSG definitions (Cassini, UTM 37N, WGS84) or custom CRS strings — for general work without sheet data.

Traverse Adjustment computes latitudes and departures, checks misclosure on closed traverses, applies Compass or Transit rule balancing, and propagates station coordinates.

Area & Perimeter calculates parcel area (m², hectares, acres) and boundary length from ordered coordinate vertices, with a map showing edge distances.

Bearing & Distance supports polar (forward) and join (inverse) calculations between survey points, with latitude/departure components and a map preview.
